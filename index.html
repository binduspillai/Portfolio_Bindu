About Me

My professional journey began in 1999 as an educator, where I spent 7 years teaching Mathematics and Computer Science in various schools, engineering colleges, and polytechnics in Mumbai. This foundational experience in education developed my ability to explain complex concepts clearly and mentor others.

In 2008, I transitioned into technology, bringing my analytical mindset and teaching skills into the data engineering field. My expertise spans across Databricks, Python, DBT, and Snowflake, with deep experience in Retail, Banking & Insurance, and Life Science domains.

My unique combination of educational background and technical expertise enables me to not only deliver complex data solutions but also effectively mentor teams and communicate insights across all organizational levels.
